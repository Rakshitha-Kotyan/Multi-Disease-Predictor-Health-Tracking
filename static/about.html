About

Health Assist is a lightweight, educational tool that demonstrates AI-assisted symptom checking and basic health tracking.

Our Mission

To make health information more approachable by pairing clean, modern interfaces with transparent machine learning outputs. We believe people should have easy access to tools that help them make informed decisions and seek timely professional care.

What this app does

  • Lets you select symptoms and see a ranked list of possible conditions with confidence scores.
  • Shows plain-language descriptions and practical precautions for many conditions.
  • Includes a simple Health Tracking page to record demo metrics like heart rate, steps, and sleep.

The predictions are generated by a trained model using the dataset included in the repository. All processing happens on the server and no data is stored in this demo.

Important disclaimer

This application is for educational demonstration only and is not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the guidance of qualified health professionals with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
